# The Importance of Cultural Sensitivity in Translation

Translation is more than just swapping words from one language to another. It's about conveying a message accurately within a specific cultural context. At Inspire Translations, we call this "Cultural Resonance."

## Why Direct Translation Fails

A literal translation of an English idiom into Swahili might make no sense, or worse, it could be offensive. Every culture has its own professional etiquette, humor, and sensitivities.

## Saving Your Reputation

1. **Avoid Embarrassment**: Many global brands have faced backlash for culturally insensitive translations.
2. **Build Trust**: When your audience feels your message was written specifically for them, they are more likely to trust you.
3. **Respect Local Norms**: Especially in sectors like legal and healthcare, respecting local norms is essential for project success.
