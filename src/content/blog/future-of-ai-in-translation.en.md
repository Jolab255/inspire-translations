# The Future of AI in Professional Translation

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly changing the landscape of many industries, and translation is no exception. Machine Translation (MT) tools are now more powerful than ever.

## The Rise of Machine Translation

AI tools like Neural Machine Translation have made it possible to translate vast amounts of text in seconds. This is excellent for low-stakes content like internal emails or basic product descriptions.

## Why Humans are Still Essential

1. **Context and Nuance**: AI still struggles with idioms, sarcasm, and complex professional terminology.
2. **Confidentiality**: Professional human translators provide a level of data security and ethical oversight that AI cannot match.
3. **Cultural Adaptation**: AI translates words; humans translate culture.
